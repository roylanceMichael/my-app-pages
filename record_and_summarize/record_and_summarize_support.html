Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get a Gemini API Key?

You can generate a free API key from Google AI Studio.

  • Go to https://aistudio.google.com/app/apikey.
  • Create a new key.
  • Open the app Settings, paste the key, and click Save.

Where are my recordings saved?

Your recordings and summaries are stored locally on your device in your secure documents folder. They are not uploaded to any cloud server (other than the text sent temporarily to Gemini for summarization).

Why does the app need Microphone permission?

The app's primary function is to record audio for summarization. We only access the microphone when you explicitly tap the "Record" button.

How do I customize the summary style?

Tap the Edit Note icon in the top right corner of the main screen. You can enable Custom Prompt and type instructions like "Summarize as a bulleted list" or "Focus on action items."

What happens if I close the app while recording?

On Desktop: The recording will stop if the app process is terminated.

On Mobile (Android/iOS): The app runs a foreground service, so recording will continue even if you lock your screen or switch to another app.
