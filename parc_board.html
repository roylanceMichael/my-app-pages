IMPORTANT NOTICE: Insurance Deductible Change

Effective October 1, 2025, the HOA Master Policy deductible for property damage has increased to $100,000.

Unit owners are now personally responsible for this amount for any loss affecting their unit. It is critical that you contact your personal insurance agent immediately.

Action Required

Update your HO-6 Policy to include:

  • $100,000 Dwelling Coverage (Coverage A)
  • $10,000 Loss Assessment Coverage

New Utah HOA Ombudsman Office

The Department of Commerce has established a new Office of the Homeowners' Association Ombudsman. This neutral office helps owners and boards understand state laws and provides advisory opinions to resolve disputes before legal action.

New State Registry Requirement

A new mandatory registry replaces the old system (shutting down 10/31/25).
Good news: PMSI will handle all registration compliance and fees automatically for our community.
